About Lore Olympus

Lore Olympus is a modern retelling of the classic myth of Hades and Persephone. It delves into the complexities of their relationship, exploring themes of love, betrayal, and self-discovery in a contemporary setting. With its stunning artwork and captivating storytelling, Lore Olympus has garnered a massive following, making it one of the top webtoons in the world.
